Aerial Burial

Summer 2020, DALI Lab

Aerial Burial's web platform provides drone-based ash scattering services and the personal and customizable memorialization of loved ones. The service facilitates funeral and scattering planning along with virtual obituaries, while comforting a grieving user after the passing of a family member or friend.

Understanding the Context

Why Aerial Burial?

Aerial Burial was brought by a graduate student after winning a pitch competition to Dartmouth College's DALI Lab, an innovation lab for undergraduate student designers, developers, and project managers. The goal was to design a unique service to transform the current funerary and ash scattering options, providing a more affordable, customizable, and peaceful scattering of ashes.

The Problem

How might we design an easy and comforting experience for learning about and planning a drone ash scattering and other funerary services?
