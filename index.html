My education is my most prized possession. I have always been fortunate enough to come from a family that cherishes the right and privilege of having an education. My parents' and older brother's studies have inspired me throughout my life, and I consistenly strive to perform the best that I can given the opportunities that I am lucky to have.

So, to me, my education is not a few words and numbers glued together, but rather it is each experience I gain from my time inside and outside of the classroom. The gift of an education cannot be summed up on a webpage. But I can assure you that some of the most liberating moments of my life have involved solving complex problems and feeling the thrill of learning, or connecting the joy on someone's face to the little flutter I get in my heart when they learn something from me. I hope to continue learning and helping others learn for the rest of my life.
