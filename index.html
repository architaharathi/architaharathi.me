Past

2017 - A book!!

In the beginning of my senior year of high school, I published a book describing my life as an Indian-American teenager called Crayon Box (on Amazon).

To me, publishing was not even the best part! I was able to take my book to my old middle school and also to my high school staff, where I presented to bring awareness of the experiences I underwent as a first-generation American influenced by media and what society says is "beautiful". It was the perfect way for me to give back to my wonderful communities and to hopefully inspire and pave the way for future generations (or their educators!) to change how society accepts diversity.
